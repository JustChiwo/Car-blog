The BMW E36 3 Series

This is where it starts. The E36 is old-school cool without trying too hard. Boxy but smooth, lightweight, and raw enough to actually feel connected to the road. It’s obtainable, moddable, and still respected at car meets. Most common engines range from about 134–168 hp stock, but with mods, these cars wake up fast. This is the perfect first real enthusiast car.
